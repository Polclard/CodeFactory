Exercise 2:

Title: Path Finder

Find the movement pattern for 'P' to get to 'X'.
You need to write how much fields should 'P' needs to move Up, Down, Left or Right to get to 'X'


Example:


Up: 0
Down: 8
Left: 0
Right: 8

P - - - - - - - - -
- - - - - - - - - -
- - - - - - - - - -
- - - - - - - - - -
- - - - - - - - - -
- - - - - - - - - -
- - - - - - - - - -
- - - - - - - - - -
- - - - - - - - X -
- - - - - - - - - -


Explanation:
'P' needs to go Down: 8 fields, and than it needs to go Right: 8 fields.
